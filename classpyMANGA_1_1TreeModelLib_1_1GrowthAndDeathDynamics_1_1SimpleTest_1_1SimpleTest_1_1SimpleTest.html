pyMANGA.TreeModelLib.GrowthAndDeathDynamics.SimpleTest.SimpleTest.SimpleTest.trees

This functions prepares the cgrowth and death concept.

In the SimpleTest concept, trees are saved in a simple list and the timestepping is updated. In preparation for the next time- step, the list is simply resetted. : t_ini - initial time for next timestep
t_end - end time for next timestep
